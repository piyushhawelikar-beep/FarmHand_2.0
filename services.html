Our Services

FarmHand provides a variety of services to support farmers and labourers in agriculture.

👨‍🌾 Farmer Job Posting

Farmers can post job requirements like harvesting, ploughing, and irrigation.

🧑‍🔧 Labour Job Search

Labourers can find jobs nearby and earn fair wages.

💰 Digital Payment History

Track wages and payments digitally for transparency and trust.

🌦 Weather & Soil Advisory

Get weather updates and crop advice based on soil type & location.

🔔 Notifications

Get instant reminders for sowing, harvesting, and payments.

🌍 Multilingual Support

Use the app in your own language with voice input support.
